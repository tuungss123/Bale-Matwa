About Us

Holy Angel University is a private Catholic university located in Angeles City, Pampanga, Philippines. It was founded in 1933 by the late Don Juan Nepomuceno as a high school for boys, and later expanded to include college programs. Throughout its history, the university has faced several challenges, including the destruction of its campus during the eruption of Mount Pinatubo in 1991. However, the university was able to rebuild and continue its mission of providing quality education to students from all walks of life. Today, Holy Angel University is recognized as one of the top universities in the Philippines, offering a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields of study.
